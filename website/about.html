My Journey

AI Product Manager with a passion for innovation

LinkedIn

My journey into AI product management began with a fascination for how technology can augment human capabilities. With a background in both computer science and business strategy, I found my calling at the intersection of artificial intelligence and product development.

Over the years, I've had the privilege of working with innovative companies and talented teams to build AI products that have impacted millions of users. From conversational AI to predictive analytics, computer vision to natural language processing, I've explored diverse AI domains and learned that the key to successful AI products lies not just in the technology, but in deeply understanding user needs and business objectives.

Today, I focus on helping organizations navigate the complex landscape of AI product development, ensuring that AI solutions are not only technically sound but also ethically responsible, user-friendly, and commercially viable.

Areas of Expertise

AI & Machine Learning

Deep understanding of various AI/ML technologies including natural language processing, computer vision, predictive analytics, and generative AI. Experienced in translating complex ML concepts into product features that deliver tangible value.

Product Management

End-to-end product lifecycle management from ideation to launch and beyond. Expert in product strategy, roadmap planning, feature prioritization, and stakeholder management. Skilled at balancing technical feasibility, business viability, and user desirability.

User Research & Design

Strong advocate for user-centered design in AI products. Experienced in conducting user research, creating personas, mapping user journeys, and working closely with UX designers to create intuitive AI-powered experiences.

Data Analytics

Proficient in using data to drive product decisions. Skilled in defining key metrics, conducting A/B tests, analyzing user behavior, and leveraging insights to improve product performance and user satisfaction.
