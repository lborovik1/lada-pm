My Journey

Lada Borovikova, Ph.D. LinkedIn

My journey into machine learnong (ML) and artificial intelligence (AI) started from neuroscience. I was passioned about how brains and neural system works. With development of neural networks and deep learning, AI technologies became available for people and companies. My IT management experience began with a fascination for how technology can augment human capabilities. With a background in both computer science and business, I found my calling at the intersection of artificial intelligence and product development.

Over the years, I've had the privilege of working with innovative companies and talented teams to build products that have impacted millions of users. From chip development projects to conversational AI agents and predictive analytics, computer vision to natural language processing, I've explored diverse different domains and learned that the key to successful AI products lies not just in the technology, but in deeply understanding user needs and business objectives.

Today, I focus on helping organizations navigate the complex landscape of AI product development, ensuring that AI solutions are not only technically sound but also ethically responsible, user-friendly, and commercially viable.
